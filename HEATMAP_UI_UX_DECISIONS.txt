CURRENT PROBLEMS (from HEARDROP):

NEW DESIGN (Tripomatic-inspired):

1. MAP STYLE
   - Richer colors with visible green parks
   - Dark mode default BUT with better contrast
   - Street names visible at zoom levels
   - Consider light mode option for daytime use

2. PIN DESIGN
   - Circular pins with LOGO or PHOTO inside
   - White border around circle for visibility
   - Size varies by zoom level (larger when zoomed in)
   - Clustered pins when zoomed out (show count)
   - Selected pin gets highlight ring (pulsing or glowing)

3. SCREEN REAL ESTATE
   - NO permanent header on map view
   - City selector chip at top center ("London" with 3D icon)
   - Map controls on right edge (zoom +/-, layers, location)
   - Everything else in collapsible panels/sheets
